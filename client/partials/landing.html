Instant Personality

Whether you're making conversational personas for entertainment, education, healthcare, training, or just for fun, adding autonomous emotions and personality will make your characters more engaging and effective.

Stylized or Photorealistic Characters

We specialize in the physics of emotion; the way that characters move and express themselves. Research has shown that photorealistic virtual humans can lead to positive outcomes in healthcare, education, and training. Our technology adapts to make sure your character's identity comes through.

Personal Eye Contact

Eye contact increases attention and makes people feel more connected to your characters. They will blink normally, occasionally avert their gaze, and move in ways that feel natural. In VR, this has an especially powerful impact.

About

The medium of interactive entertainment has barely begun to take shape. Game developers have made beautiful experiences but game engines are heavily skewed toward creating navigation-based games. We believe that communicative and social applications will drive widespread adoption of VR & AR even more than games. Truly compelling virtual characters in this new medium will require a new kind of interactivity engine that prioritizes communication and emotional expression.

Right now there's a well documented content vacuum in VR that is similar to the vacuum that slowed public acceptance in the early days of television. We enable artists, researchers, business developers and others to rapidly create characters and experiences for this new medium.

Our team members have led companies which licensed prior animation software to NASA, MIT, Sony Pictures, Intel, Microsoft, Boston Dynamics, and several universities. That software has been used in hundreds of scientific studies that investigate human responses to virtual humans.

Now we're solving a missing link in VR, AR, and mixed reality.
